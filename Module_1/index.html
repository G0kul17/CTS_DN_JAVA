Community Bulletin Board  — Latest notices from City Hall

Road Closure Notice

Main Street will be closed between Oak Avenue and Elm Drive from June 10–12 for pavement resurfacing. Residents are advised to use Riverside Road as an alternative.

Park Renovation Update

The renovation of Central Park's east wing is on schedule. The new amphitheatre and community garden will open for the Summer Fair on June 14th. Volunteers are welcome.

Library Extended Hours

Maplewood Public Library will stay open until 9 PM on Tuesdays and Thursdays throughout June to support exam season. Free study rooms can be booked online via the council website.

Recycling Scheme Launch

The new food-waste programme launches June 1st. Green caddy bins are being delivered to all households by May 30th. Collections will occur every Tuesday alongside regular refuse collection.

Voter Registration Reminder

The local ward election is July 4th. Register to vote by June 10th at the Electoral Registration Office or online. Polling stations open 7 AM–10 PM.
